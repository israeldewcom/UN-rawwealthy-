Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I start investing?

To start investing, simply register for an account, verify your identity through our KYC process, choose an investment plan that suits your goals, and make your first deposit. The entire process takes less than 10 minutes.

When will I receive my returns?

Returns are calculated daily and credited to your account at the end of each business day. You can withdraw your earnings or reinvest them for compound growth. The first returns are typically paid 24 hours after your investment is approved.

What is the minimum investment?

The minimum investment is 3,500 for most of our plans. We offer tiered plans to accommodate different investment levels and goals. Some premium plans may have higher minimums.

How secure is my investment?

We employ bank-level security measures including SSL encryption, two-factor authentication, and segregated client accounts. Your investments are protected by our comprehensive security protocols and insured against unauthorized access.

Are there any withdrawal fees?

Yes, we charge a 5% platform fee on all withdrawals to cover transaction costs and platform maintenance. This fee is automatically deducted from your withdrawal amount. There are no hidden fees.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawal requests are processed within 24-48 hours during business days. Bank transfers typically take 1-3 business days to reflect in your account, depending on your bank. Crypto withdrawals are usually faster.
